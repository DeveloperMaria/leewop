COMMON QUESTIONS & MISCONCEPTIONS IN DCAA COMPLIANT ACCOUNTING
  • There is no such thing as a "DCAA-approved accounting software"
    • Meaning, you don't have to use one program or another to make sure your accounting is DCAA compliant. The DCAA cares more about the compliance of the overall accounting system rather than whether you use PROCAS or Deltek or QuickBooks.
  • Interested in QuickBooks for government accounting?
    • We use QuickBooks with ICAT and other third party applications to create a compliant accounting system for our government contractor clients.

  • There are many different types of DCAA accounting audits
    • Depending on your type of contract, your accounting system and your current status, you need the right support to prepare for your DCAA audit.
