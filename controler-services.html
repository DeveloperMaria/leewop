CONTROLLER SERVICES

Our outsourced controller services are specifically designed to suit the exact needs of growing businesses. With a focus on businesses with a minimum of $2 million in annual revenue (and scaling to $100+ million and higher), our controllers are responsible for overseeing all client accounts, providing timely insights and financial advice, and carrying out proactive account management, a standard of excellence at AccountingDepartment.com.

All AccountingDepartment.com Controllers are CPAs with extensive experience in financial management and controller services. They oversee all work completed by our full-time bookkeepers, close client books on a monthly or quarterly basis and provide consultations on a wide variety of client matters.

Some of the specific responsibilities our outsourced controller services offer to clients:

  • Management of accounting and bookkeeping processes
  • Budgeting and forecasting
  • Financial reporting
  • Cash flow management and projections
  • Accounting documentation, including processes, procedures, and operations manuals
  • Job costing and inventory tracking
  • Key metric benchmarking
  • Third-party accounting software selection and implementation
  • Reporting and oversight of month-end closings
  • Audit preparation and advisory service
  • Management of accounting and bookkeeping processes
  • Establishing Internal Controls
  • Developing KPIs and Measuring Against Benchmarks
  • Financial Strategy & Strategic Planning
  • Monitoring marketing spend & tracking against budgets
  • Overseeing accounting staff
  • Advising on financial decisions
