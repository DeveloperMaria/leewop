CFO / Controller Affiliate Program

CFOs and Controllers work with AccountingDepartment.com because the integrity of the financial data they receive is critical to making operational decisions. Whether you're a full-time CFO looking for bookkeeping or transactional support, a part-time or interim CFO requiring the involvement of controller oversight, or a Controller looking for back office bookkeeping support, AccountingDepartment.com puts the data you need right in your hands.

CFOs and Controllers working with AccountingDepartment.com benefit from bookkeeping and/or controller services that work to provide them with:

    • Full back office bookkeeping support
    • Accurate, timely and thorough financial statements on due dates you determine
    • Controller oversight to assist with forecast and budget projections
    • A documented procedure manual including reporting dates, deadlines and turnaround times
    • A seamless partnership with a bookkeeper and controller team
    • Clear and consistent communication

Our CFO Affiliate Program can be custom-tailored to your firm's unique needs. We typically offer a recurring revenue stream as well as other perks.
