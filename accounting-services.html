About AccountingDepartment.com

Providing established and growing businesses with the best in outsourced accounting services since 2004.

When it comes to your business's critical financial data, you need to trust your bookkeeper. AccountingDepartment.com employs staff throughout the U.S. as well as in the Philippines. We recruit and hire only those who pass comprehensive tests and stringent requirements so every employee is held to the same standards of excellence regardless of their country of residence. All clients have a virtual accounting team comprised of full-time AccountingDepartment.com employees, so your financial records are never passed on to subcontractors.

All our virtual accountants, financial controllers and implementation team members have the luxury of working from their own homes, from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. in their designated time zone. Because we are a paperless environment, our virtual accounting team logs in to AccountingDepartment.com servers, where all work is processed and completed.

When you team up with AccountingDepartment.com, your information isn't passed out to just anybody. There's no call center. You are assigned a dedicated accounting team who works for you on a daily basis. Your accounting specialist in particular is available by phone, email or instant messenger (IM via Zoom).
